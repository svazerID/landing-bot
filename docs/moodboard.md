# Visual Direction and Tokens

## Design Intent

- Keep a warm, minimal, typography-forward style.
- Prioritize readability and quick scanning.
- Use restrained card styling and subtle interactions.

## Typography

- Heading: `"Fraunces", Georgia, serif`
- Body: `"Inter", "Segoe UI", sans-serif`
- Scale:
  - `--fs-hero: clamp(2rem, 4vw, 3.25rem)`
  - `--fs-h2: clamp(1.4rem, 2.2vw, 2rem)`
  - `--fs-body: 1rem`
  - `--fs-meta: 0.9rem`

## Color Tokens

- `--color-bg: #f7f6f3`
- `--color-surface: #ffffff`
- `--color-text: #1f2328`
- `--color-muted: #56606b`
- `--color-border: #d9dee5`
- `--color-accent: #2f6fed`
- `--color-accent-soft: #eaf0ff`

## Spacing Tokens

- `--space-2: 0.5rem`
- `--space-3: 0.75rem`
- `--space-4: 1rem`
- `--space-6: 1.5rem`
- `--space-8: 2rem`
- `--space-12: 3rem`

## Component Rules

- Cards: 1px border, 12px radius, light shadow on hover.
- Tags: rounded pills, compact size, low-contrast background.
- Sections: clear heading + concise content block.
- Links: underline on hover/focus for clarity.
